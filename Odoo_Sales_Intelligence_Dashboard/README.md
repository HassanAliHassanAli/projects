## 📌 Executive Summary
Raw data from ERP systems like **Odoo** is often overwhelming and difficult to interpret at a glance. This project delivers an End-to-End Business Intelligence solution that extracts, transforms, and visualizes sales data to drive executive decision-making. 

By building a specialized data pipeline and an interactive Power BI suite, I transformed transactional records into a **"Single Source of Truth"** for sales performance, regional growth, and team efficiency.

---

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
The project follows a professional data lifecycle:
1.  **Source:** Transactional data extracted from Odoo ERP modules.
2.  **Storage & ETL:** Data organized and cleaned (using SQL/PostgreSQL) to ensure consistency.
3.  **Modeling:** Developed a relational Star Schema in Power BI to handle multi-currency and multi-region sales.
4.  **Visualization:** Designed a dual-layered dashboard (Executive vs. Operations) for different stakeholder needs.

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue:** $5.23M

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
Beyond the charts, I extracted the following actionable business strategies:
* **High-Margin Focus:** Since the average margin is 27.24%, focus marketing efforts on the **"Services"** category which historically shows higher profitability than physical products.
* **Regional Expansion:** Invest in the **Portugal and USA** markets, as they represent the highest revenue clusters with a combined value exceeding 50% of total sales.
* **Team Optimization:** Implement a mentorship program where top-performing reps (based on the dashboard) share tactics with the lower-performing quartiles to normalize sales efficiency.
