## 📌 Executive Summary
In the complex world of global commerce, supply chain efficiency directly dictates profitability. This project leverages the **DataCo Smart Supply Chain dataset** to uncover logistical bottlenecks, evaluate delivery performance, and analyze global profit margins. 

Using **Tableau**, I developed interactive visual dashboards that transform raw logistical data into a strategic asset, enabling supply chain managers to identify exactly where delays occur and which regions yield the highest returns.

---

## 🎯 Key Dashboards & Business Insights

### 1. Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard
A comprehensive view of the operational health and fulfillment efficiency.
* **Delivery Status Overview:** Highlighted a critical operational bottleneck by tracking the exact proportion of **"Late Deliveries"** versus "On-Time" and "Advance Shipping".
* **Lead Time Distribution:** Monitored the average lead time to identify delays in the fulfillment cycle.
* **Category & Regional Revenue:** Identified top-performing product categories (e.g., Fishing, Cleats, Camping) and mapped revenue generation across key regions like Western Europe and Central America.

### 2. Global Sales & Profitability Map
A geospatial analysis of the company's global footprint.
* **Visualizing Margins:** Mapped sales volume against profit margins globally, revealing that high revenue does not always equal high profitability in certain logistics routes.
* **Strategic Routing:** Provided a visual heatmap to help stakeholders re-evaluate shipping routes and logistics partnerships in low-margin regions.

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
Based on the Tableau visualizations, I propose the following operational improvements:
* **Mitigate Late Deliveries:** Investigate the root causes of the "Late Delivery" segment. Implement stricter SLAs (Service Level Agreements) with carriers operating in regions with the highest delay frequencies.
* **Optimize High-Margin Routes:** Reallocate marketing and logistical resources towards regions showing the highest "Profit Margin by Region" to maximize ROI.
* **Inventory Balancing:** Align warehouse stock levels with the top-performing categories (Fishing, Cleats) to ensure rapid fulfillment and reduce lead times.
