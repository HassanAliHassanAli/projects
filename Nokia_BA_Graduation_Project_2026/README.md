## 📌 Executive Summary
This repository contains the complete documentation and executive presentation for my fourth-year graduation project. The project is an in-depth **Business and Data Analysis of Nokia**, focusing on its historical market shifts, internal systems, current competitive landscape, and strategic roadmaps.

The goal of this analysis is to evaluate Nokia's business model transitions and provide data-driven recommendations for its future market positioning using comprehensive strategic frameworks.

---

## 🏗️ Core Analysis Areas
* **Strategic Frameworks:** Comprehensive SWOT Analysis, pinpointing Nokia's internal strengths and vulnerabilities against external market opportunities and threats.
* **Competitive Landscape:** Evaluation of Nokia's market share and positioning against major telecommunications and tech competitors.
* **Data Visualization:** Translating complex market data and internal performance metrics into clear, actionable visual insights using **Power BI**.
* **Business Model Evaluation:** A deep dive into Nokia's transition from consumer electronics to B2B enterprise solutions and cloud infrastructure.
